## Description
CODEX·IA is a Mexican Law MCP (Model Context Protocol) server that indexes the complete Mexican legal corpus — 59,000+ documents across 34 branches of law — and exposes it as AI-native tools. Unlike traditional legal databases that serve content through web interfaces, CODEX·IA provides structured programmatic access via MCP, enabling Claude, Cursor, and any compatible AI assistant to query Mexican legislation directly.

Key features: Full-text search with sub-100ms latency (SQLite FTS5), verbatim article retrieval with zero LLM summarization, DOF reform drift detection, and a browsable statute catalog. Open source under Apache 2.0.

CODEX·IA addresses the legal AI hallucination problem at the infrastructure level: by providing AI models with direct access to exact statutory text, it eliminates the need for the model to rely on training data for legal citations.
